About Us

Delivering Engineering, Procurement and Construction for Clients around the World DecoHF Group is one of the world's largest publicly-traded engineering, procurement, construction (EPC) and maintenance companies, providing innovative EPC services to government and private-sector Clients in diverse industries. For over a century, Clients have trusted DecoHF Group to successfully, ethically and safely complete their capital projects.
