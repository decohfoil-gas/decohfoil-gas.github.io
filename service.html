Oil & Gas Products & Services

DecoHF Group has been supplying and producing a wide range of Oil & Gas products, as well as drilling fluids, minerals and chemicals to the oil and gas industry for more than a decade.

Supported by advanced pumping equipment, DecoHF Group provides its clients with progressive and robust solutions to solve the challenges they face in the oil and gas industry. DecoHF Group has become an important regional player in the Middle-East, with a depth of experience and a commitment to technological innovation, performance and environmentally responsible solutions across all five of our service lines - stimulation, cementing, coiled tubing, filtration and pipeline services. Through our extensive experience in large-scale operations, combined with recent advancements such as flexible cement, together with our ongoing research into environmentally responsible solutions and advanced technologies, we strive to influence the industry while meeting the needs of our clients.

Stimulation : DecoHF Group Stimulation and Pumping Services are provided in all areas of operation, utilizing modern technology and advanced equipment.

Coiled Tubing : With state-of-the-art technology, DecoHF Group can provide a suite of regular and advanced applications of Coiled Tubing Services for both onshore and offshore fields.

Cementing : With strong HSEQ standards, and with an arsenal of modern equipment and professional human resources, DecoHF Group Cementing Services have risen to the challenges and met client expectations on every single job.

Filtration : DecoHF Group provides filtration services to its clients to meet with their requirement, saving the additional cost of well stimulation and avoiding related operational problems due to non-filtered fluids, especially across the pay zone.

Pipeline Services : DecoHF Group provides a suite of Pipeline Services to refineries and plants, including water filling & hydro testing, nitrogen de-cruding, nitrogen de-watering & drying, nitrogen purging - cutting/welding, nitrogen de-gassing, cooling down piping, and Pressure Testing.

Oil Drilling

An oil well is a boring in the Earth that is designed to bring petroleum oil hydrocarbons to the surface. Usually some natural gas is released along with the oil. A well that is designed to produce only gas may be termed a gas well.

Gas Flares

An gas flare, alternatively known as a flare stack, is a gas combustion device used in industrial plants such as petroleum refineries, chemical plants, natural gas processing plants as well as at oil or gas production sites having oil wells, gas wells, offshore oil and gas rigs and landfills.

Oil Refineries

An oil refinery or petroleum refinery is an industrial process plant where crude oil is transformed and refined into more useful products such as petroleum naphtha, gasoline, diesel fuel, asphalt base, heating oil, kerosene, liquefied petroleum gas, jet fuel and fuel oils. 炼油厂或炼油厂是将原油转化并提炼成更有用的产品（例如石脑油， Liànyóu chǎng huò liànyóu chǎng shì jiāng yuányóu zhuǎnhuà bìng tíliàn

oil platform & drilling rig

An oil platform, offshore platform, or offshore drilling rig is a large structure with facilities for well drilling to explore, extract, store, and process petroleum and natural gas which lies in rock formations beneath the seabed. Many oil platforms will also contain facilities to accommodate their workforce.

A drilling rig is an integrated system that drills wells, such as oil or water wells, in the earth's subsurface.
